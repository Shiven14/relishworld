Our Story

Where Fast Food
Meets Soul

Relish World was born in 2002 from a simple belief: fast food should taste like it was made with love. Located in the heart of Anand, we've spent over two decades crafting vegetarian fast food that brings the whole family together.

Every sandwich is grilled to golden perfection. Every dosa made fresh to order. Every bhaji simmered with the kind of care you can taste in every single bite.
